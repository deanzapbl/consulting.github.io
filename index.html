Our Programs

Four Pathways to
STEAM Excellence

From hands-on science lessons for young learners to career launch resources for teens — we meet every student where they are.

🧪
Ages 4–14

STEAM Enrichment

Our flagship program uses the 5E instructional model — Engage, Explore, Explain, Elaborate, Evaluate — to deliver hands-on lessons in Astronomy, Biology, Chemistry, Earth Science, Engineering, and Physics. Lessons turn into artworks that are as pleasing to the eye as they are intriguing to the mind.

Explore program
🧭
Middle School

Career Compass

Career Compass helps students explore the intersection of their passions and STEAM fields. Through career assessments, mentorship sessions, and industry exposure, students build the clarity and confidence to envision their professional future early.

Explore program
🏛️
All Ages

Young STEAMers Academy

A structured, multi-session academy experience that deepens STEAM knowledge over an extended curriculum. Students engage in project-based challenges, collaborative problem-solving, and community showcase events that celebrate their work.

Explore program
🚀
High School & Beyond

NextGen LaunchPad

LaunchPad is your pathway to a STEAM future. Students Explore careers via quizzes and personality assessments, Prepare with resume, cover letter, and interview coaching, and Experience real-world opportunities through internships, apprenticeships, and part-time positions.

Mission, Vision & Values

Education is the Gateway to Every Child's Future

Young STEAMers' mission is to ignite the imaginations of under-resourced children by providing hands-on STEAM enrichment activities that inspire, educate, and empower.

Our Vision: Creating a world with equitable access to STEAM careers for all youth.

Respect
We treat others with dignity and acknowledge their unique contributions.
Equity
We provide equal opportunities irrespective of socioeconomic background.
Empowerment
We encourage and guide each other to reach our fullest potential.
Innovation
We approach challenges with fearless curiosity and creative thinking.
